# CASSI-256x256x28 T1 Nominal

## Dataset

10 hyperspectral scenes, 256x256x28, with a fixed coded aperture.
Measurements simulated via the calibrated Phi with sigma = 0.01.

## Metrics

- **PSNR**: per-scene channel-mean PSNR (dB), reported as the mean over
  the test split.
- **SSIM**: per-scene channel-mean SSIM.

## Baselines

| Method | PSNR (dB) | SSIM |
|---|---|---|
| GAP-TV (60 iters) | 21.5 | 0.55 |
| DGSMP (pretrained) | 32.0 | 0.91 |
